The Sinner's Prayer

Heavenly Father, I believe you sent Your son, Jesus Christ, to die for me on the cross of Calvary, and He rose again on the 3rd day.
I repent of all my sins, and I receive Jesus into my heart as my Lord, and my savior,
Fill me, Lord, with Your Holy Spirit. Help me to work in holiness, And righteousness, In Jesus's Name,
Amen!
